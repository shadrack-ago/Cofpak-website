Welcome to Confraternity of patients

The Confraternity of Patients Kenya (Cofpak) is a registered non-profit organization
as per the Laws of Kenya oriented to the
health and social well-being of the public in Kenya.
Our mandate is to advance, represent, safeguard and promote
the interests of healthcare services seekers at all levels.
COFPAK is a voluntary citizen’s organization
and is ardently independent of political or religious persuasion.
